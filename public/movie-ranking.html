The Best Movies According to Kyle

My top 3 movies of all-time:

Home Alone

This is number one on my list of favorite movies. This movie is so nostalgic for me and brings back many memories of my childhood. The Christmas setting makes it a perfect movie to watch during the holidays.

The Help

I'm a huge fan of historical fiction, so this movie is at the top of my list. Segregation is a huge part of our history and this movie puts that all into perspective.

Finding Nemo

There's just something about adorable fish and Disney movies that I can't get seem to stop watching. Disney movies also bring me back to my childhood and this movie is one of those.
